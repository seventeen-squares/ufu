Apartments

Trust Ufulu Gardens to satisfy your hospitality needs for your extended stay experience, while travelling for business or holiday, with our fully furnished, serviced apartments. Ranging from one to three bedroom apartments, our apartments are ideal for long stay residents, but also provide the perfect base for a shorter trip. Make the most of the convenience offered by a furnished apartment with a fully equipped kitchen and spacious rooms and private veranda while we take care of the housekeeping duties.

For those who are seeking an extended-stay, have extended families, or simply want a home-away-from-home, Ufulu Gardens caters to those needs by offering one, two, and three-bedroom accommodation, all offered on a nightly, weekly, monthly, or yearly basis.
